ITU Request

ITU Request for Primary User Frequency Allocation

ITU_RR2008-VolIII-e445-446.pdf
ITU-R WRC-07 Resolution 953, to initiate studies as to whether to SRDs (including RFID) should be permitted in ISM bands.

Herein provided are the ITU-R Radio Regulations Terms and Definitions from Volume I, Article I

1.3 telecommunication: Any transmission, emission or reception of signs, signals, writings, images and sounds or intelligence of any nature by wire, radio, optical or other electromagnetic systems (CS).

1.15 industrial, scientific and medical (ISM) applications (of radio frequency energy): Operation of equipment or appliances designed to generate and use locally radio frequency energy for industrial, scientific, medical, domestic or similar purposes, excluding applications in the field of telecommunications.

ITU_Res54_RA07_R-VADM-RES-2007.pdf
Resolution 54 (WRC07) for ITU to 1)continue its studies to enable implementation of advanced technologies for SRDs, thereby in particular focusing on a strategy for the future; and 2) that in particular the following studies should be conducted: a) to collect information on SRDs which use advanced spectrum access and frequency tuning range techniques in order to understand their capabilities, meanwhile ensuring protection to radiocommunication services; b) to advise on a mechanism, based on 2 a) above, that may ease the use of relevant frequency bands and/or frequency tuning ranges, preferably on a global or regional basis, suitable for SRDs.
