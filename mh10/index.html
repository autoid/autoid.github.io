ANSI MH10

The scope of MH10 is as follows:
"To facilitate freight movement within transportation and distribution systems by providing standards for transport-packages and unit-loads, including their dimensions, definitions, terminology, coding, labeling, and performance criteria; and to represent the United States' interests within the scope of ISO/TC 122"

MH10 is the US Technical Advisory Group to ANSI for ISO TC-122 Packaging
Material Handling Industry serves as Secretariat for MH10.
