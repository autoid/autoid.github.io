Why advertise with autoid.org?

 

For the first time, autoid.org is accepting third party advertising.  autoid.org has been a document portal for national and international standards development for over 10 years. Since migrating to more powerful software, autoid.org also offers live updates, RSS feeds, state of the art forums for discussions, and the ability to create polls that can be used to determine the popularity or disparity on any subject related to automatic identification.

 

autoid.org has a document archive exceeding 20,000 documents, from over 20 automatic identification standards bodies.  autoid.org has been referenced in ANSI’s (American National Standards Institute) training materials as a valuable resource for those in the AutoID industry.

 

Take advantage of the thousands of weekly web visits from businesses globally by selecting one of autoid.org’s advertising options.

 

 

Advertising placement

 

  • Every page on autoid.org will display your advertising choice, whether it is a banner, large or small graphic, or text.
  • Banners located at the top of the pages will be rotated every minute. 
  • Side placed graphics or links will be rotated on a regular basis to assure your advertisement will be seen as often as possible in the “top” slot.

 

Advertising content and guidelines

 

  • Graphics must be of the .jpg format. No animated gifs are permitted.
  • All graphics must be approved by the administrative staff of autoid.org
