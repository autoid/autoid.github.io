NOTICE


We regret to inform you that as a result of repeated hacking attempts, one such attack succeeded, exposing copyrighted material. As a result of this exposure, all autoid.org documents, including those previously publicly available, are now behind a web application firewall and require a userid and password for access.


If you are attempting to secure a document listed in ISO/IEC 15418:2009, please identify that document and send an email request to craig.harmon@qed.org

Mission Statement

Untitled Document

The goal for this site is to keep end users, organizations, and companies in the Automatic Identification Data Collection industry up to date with essential standards activities and technical information that can effect business decisions. autoid.org™ also offers website creation, website management and web hosting services for small and large companies and trade organizations in the automatic identification business. To get the best the web has to offer in the field of automatic identification, let autoid.org's™ expertise create your online presence. Whether you are simply looking for a place to announce your products and services, or if you are looking to create an interactive megasite offering technical information, primers, tutorials, or online commerce, become a part of the AIDC online community with autoid.org™.
